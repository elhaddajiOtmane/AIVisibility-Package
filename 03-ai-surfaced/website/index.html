FAQ

Common Questions

What is LLMO?

Large Language Model Optimization (LLMO) is like SEO for AI. It's the practice of optimizing your brand's presence so AI assistants like ChatGPT recommend you when users ask relevant questions.

How long until I see results?

Initial improvements can appear within 4–8 weeks. AI models update their knowledge bases periodically, so visibility builds over time. Most clients see significant results within 3 months.

Do you guarantee AI will recommend me?

We can't control AI outputs directly — nobody can. But we optimize every factor that influences AI recommendations. Our methodology significantly increases your chances of being mentioned.

How do you measure success?

We track brand mentions across AI platforms, test with real queries, and monitor changes over time. You'll get clear reports showing your visibility progress and competitive position.

What industries do you work with?

We focus on B2B SaaS, professional services, e-commerce, and tech companies. If people search for solutions in your category using AI, we can help.

Is this different from regular SEO?

Yes. SEO optimizes for search engine rankings. LLMO optimizes for AI recommendations. The tactics overlap somewhat, but AI models use different data sources and ranking factors than Google.
