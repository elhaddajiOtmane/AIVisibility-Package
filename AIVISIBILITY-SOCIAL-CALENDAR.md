**LinkedIn Post - Pillar 1: Educational**
```
SEO ≠ LLMO

Here's the difference:

SEO (Search Engine Optimization):
- Goal: Rank higher in Google results
- Method: Keywords, backlinks, technical optimization
- Output: Links to your website
- User action: Click to visit your site

LLMO (Large Language Model Optimization):
- Goal: Get recommended by AI assistants
- Method: Trust signals, entity optimization, content structure
- Output: Direct brand mention/recommendation
- User action: Trust the AI's suggestion

Why this matters:

Google shows you options. AI gives you answers.

When someone asks Google "best project management tool," they see 10 links and decide for themselves.

When someone asks ChatGPT the same question, they get a recommendation. A specific brand. An endorsement.

Which is more powerful?

SEO drives traffic. LLMO drives trust.

Both matter. But only one is being ignored by most marketers.

#LLMO #SEO #AISearch #Marketing #ContentStrategy
```
