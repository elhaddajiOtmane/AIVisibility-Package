# SECTION 1: PROBLEM STATEMENT

## The Problem We're Solving

**Problem:** Brands are invisible when people ask AI assistants (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude) for recommendations, and most don't know it.

**Why It's a Problem:**
- When someone asks "What's the best CRM for small businesses?" AI gives specific brand names
- If your brand isn't mentioned, you're invisible to that potential customer
- This is happening millions of times per day as AI search grows
- Most companies have no idea they're being excluded from AI recommendations
- Traditional SEO and marketing don't solve this - it requires different optimization

### High-Impact Risks

**1. Market Education Required**
- Risk: People don't know they have this problem
- Probability: 60%
- Mitigation: Content marketing showing problem, free audits as proof

**2. Results Take Time**
- Risk: AI visibility improvements aren't instant, clients get impatient
- Probability: 40%
- Mitigation: Set expectations, show progress metrics, case studies
- Residual Risk: Low

**3. Dependency on AI Platforms**
- Risk: ChatGPT/Perplexity change how they recommend brands
- Probability: 100% (they will change)
- Mitigation: Stay current, position as ongoing partnership
- Residual Risk: Low-Medium
