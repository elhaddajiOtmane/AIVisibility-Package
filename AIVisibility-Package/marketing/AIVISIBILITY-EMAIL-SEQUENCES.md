# A/B TESTING PLAN

## Priority Order (Test One at a Time)

1. **Subject Lines** - Test every email (biggest impact on open rate)
2. **CTA Copy** - Test button text variations
3. **Send Times** - Test 9 AM vs 12 PM vs 6 PM
4. **Email Length** - Test short vs long versions
5. **With/Without Social Proof** - Test testimonial inclusion

## Testing Protocol

- Minimum 100 recipients per variant before declaring winner
- Run test for 48-72 hours before calling results
- Only change one variable at a time
- Document results in testing log

# COMPLIANCE CHECKLIST

## CAN-SPAM (Required for US)

- [ ] Physical mailing address in every email footer
- [ ] Working unsubscribe link in every email (process within 10 days)
- [ ] "From" name and email are accurate (AI Visibility / your name)
- [ ] Subject lines are not deceptive or misleading
- [ ] Commercial intent is clear

## GDPR (Required for EU Recipients)

- [ ] Explicit opt-in consent obtained (checkbox not pre-checked)
- [ ] Consent documented (when, how, what they agreed to)
- [ ] Easy way to request data deletion
- [ ] Privacy policy linked and accessible
- [ ] Data processing agreement with email service provider

## CASL (Required for Canada)

- [ ] Express consent for new subscribers
- [ ] Implied consent valid for 24 months for existing relationships
- [ ] Sender identification in every email
- [ ] Working unsubscribe mechanism

**Note:** Verify all compliance with legal counsel for your specific situation.
