Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AI visibility and LLMO.

What is LLMO (Large Language Model Optimization)?
LLMO is the practice of optimizing your brand's online presence so that AI assistants like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude recommend you when users ask relevant questions. It's like SEO, but for AI systems instead of Google.
How is this different from SEO?
SEO helps you rank in Google's link-based results. LLMO helps you get recommended by AI assistants that give direct answers. The ranking factors are completely different - AI looks for trust signals, entity associations, and content structure that Google doesn't prioritize the same way.
How long until I see results?
Most clients start seeing improvements in AI citations within 60-90 days of implementing our recommendations. The timeline depends on your starting point and how quickly you can implement changes.
How do you measure AI visibility?
We track citation frequency (how often AI mentions you) and citation quality (in what context). We run the same prompts monthly and show you the trend over time. You'll see exactly when and how often you're being recommended.
Is AI visibility really that important?
36% of people will search via AI by 2028 according to Statista. ChatGPT has 200M+ weekly active users. When someone asks AI for recommendations and gets a specific brand name, that's incredibly powerful. Companies that optimize now will be entrenched before competitors catch on.
What if I already have good SEO?
Great SEO and great AI visibility are not the same thing. We've tested companies with first-page Google rankings who were completely invisible in AI search. The optimization strategies are different. SEO is still valuable - but it's no longer enough.
